Background checks

The background check on a child is the same as an adult check, this means we need to ask about criminal convictions.

Has your child ever been convicted of a criminal offence, including spent convictions, or been cautioned by the police?

You don't need to tell us about motoring offences or penalty points unless these were given in a court.
